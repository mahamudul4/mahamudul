About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of Minnesota, specializing in Recommender Systems and Information Retrieval. With over 8 years of teaching and research experience in Bangladesh, I have served as a Senior Lecturer at East West University and contributed to cutting-edge research in Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Blockchain. My work has been recognized with awards like the Best Paper at CIIR 2023 and a Publication Reward from East West University. I am passionate about leveraging AI to solve real-world problems and advancing academic research.
