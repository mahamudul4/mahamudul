About Me

I am a Research Assistant and PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, specializing in Recommender Systems and Human Computer Interactions advised by Professor Joseph A. Konstan. With over 8 years of teaching and research experience in Bangladesh, I have served as a Senior Lecturer at East West University and contributed to cutting-edge research in Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Blockchain. My work has been recognized with awards like the Best Researcher Reward, Best Paper at CIIR 2023 and a Publication Reward from East West University. I am passionate about leveraging AI to solve real-world problems and advancing academic research.
